CH32V003 HID Flasher

Flashes a .bin, .elf, or .uf2 over WebHID. The device enumerates as 1209:B003. Requires Chrome, Edge, or Opera.

  1. Pick a firmware file.
  2. Click Flash.
  3. Plug in the CH32V003 (or reset it) — the page detects it and starts flashing automatically.

First time only: plug in the board before clicking Flash so your browser can show the one-time permission prompt. After that, the three steps above are all you need.
